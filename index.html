Privacy Policy

Snakes Games does not collect any personal information from the user directly, we do not store data such as names, addresses or any other personal information.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
We use various services to host our multiplayer mode, these are PlayFab and Photon, PlayFab stores an approximate IP address of the user, this is only used for IP banning to prevent ban evasion.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Your hardware ID is sent to PlayFab, this is only used to login to your PlayFab account.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
We do not control what these services do with your data, if you have concerns about your data, please go to the provided links below to find out more about these services and how they collect your data :
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
https://playfab.com/terms/
https://dashboard.photonengine.com/account/privacyandcookiepolicy
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
If you want to delete your PlayFab account and the data attached to it, please contact sigmaboyguyso12@gmail.com and provide your username and anyone at Snakes Games will clear it as soon as possible
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
If you have questions, feel free to contact sigmaboyguyso12@gmail.com
